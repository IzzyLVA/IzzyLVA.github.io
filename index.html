About Me

Hello! I'm Izzy, a cybersecurity enthusiast with a passion for ethical hacking, penetration testing, and securing systems. My journey started as a kid, discovering exploits in games and learning how systems work. In middle school, I experienced being a victim of DDOS attacks, which motivated me to dive deeper into cybersecurity and learn how to protect myself and others from digital threats.

In high school, I took Cybersecurity 1 and 2 classes, earning my Security+ certification and gaining hands-on experience with various security tools and techniques. I also volunteered with a nonprofit organization, helping elders stay safe online by teaching them to recognize common scams and providing technical support when needed.

Today, I continue exploring network defense, malware analysis, and hands-on security projects, constantly sharpening my skills through research, experimentation, and real-world challenges.
